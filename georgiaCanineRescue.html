ABOUT OUR ORGANIZATION

Georgia Canine Rescue is a 501(c)(3) organization focusing on animal welfare and education. Aside from the rescue and rehab of voiceless animals, we also offer services to the public including full service grooming and a spay/neuter and vaccination clinic.

We offer private boarding to animal rescue organizations.
